Trying to decide which companies to book your trip with? Over the last 19 years of travel, I’ve used hundreds of companies during my travels. Some have been great, some have been just awful.
Below is a list of the ones I come back to time and time again. They are the best out there and continually offer superior deals and experiences. They are where all my travel planning and booking starts. Use them to book your trips too!

· · · · · · · Airfare · · · · · · ·

Going

Going (formerly Scott’s Cheap Flights) finds incredible flight deals and sends them directly to your inbox. If you’re flexible with your dates and destinations, you can score some amazing deals and save hundreds of dollars in the process! It’s the premier dealing subscription service in the United States.

Point.Me

This is my all-time favorite flight search engine. It always seems to find the best deals and its calendar view lets you see which days are cheapest to fly. I like it because it searches the small booking sites no one else does so no stone is left unturned. Start all your flight searches here.

Skyscanner

Point.me is a search and booking engine that helps you find the best ways to use your points and miles. It searches 30+ loyalty and 100+ airline programs to find the best value possible. As a Nomadic Matt reader, you can get 4 months free with the code NOMADICMATT4.

· · · · · · · Accommodation · · · · · · ·

Hostelworld

This is the best hostel-accommodation site out there, with the largest inventory, best search interface, and highest availability. I use it for all my hostel bookings. There are not many other options out there that compare to the scale this site has.

Booking.com

Booking.com probably has the widest selection of budget accommodation in the world (it is especially good for listings in developing countries). In my research, it consistently finds the cheapest rooms. I also like its easy-to-use interface and no-money-down policy. I book all my hotels here.

Trusted Housesitters

Trusted Housesitters is a platform that connects you with people in need of pet and house sitters. In exchange for looking after their pets or home, you’ll get access to free accommodation. It’s an amazing resource for long-term travelers looking to travel slowly.

· · · · · · · Travel Insurance · · · · · · ·

World Nomads

World Nomads offers comprehensive plans for adventurous travelers who plan to do things like hiking, kayaking, and even some extreme sports like rock climbing or bungy jumping. With 200 different activities covered, World Nomads is the best choice for active travelers.

SafetyWing

Safety Wing offers convenient and affordable plans tailored to travelers and digital nomads. It has cheap monthly plans, first-rate customer service, and an easy-to-use claims process that makes it perfect for those on the road. I use them to cover me on all my trips.

Medjet

Medjet membership is a very affordable option for those who are going to be far from home and want added evacuation coverage. If you’re looking to do adventure activities or go to remote destinations, MedJet is a must! It has helped lots of people out.
